    Mobile Money API - Africa

    Collect and disburse payments with ease from African Countries

    MoneyUnify is an easy-to-use USSD mobile money payments API that simplifies payment processes for businesses. It enables companies to collect and disburse payments from and to customers using various mobile money network providers. all in one place. This platform is widely used in Africa and other parts of the world for online payments, making it a convenient solution for businesses to manage their financial transactions. With MoneyUnify, Companies can easily integrate mobile payments into their operations and provide a seamless payment experience experience for their customers. Its user-friendly interface and reliable service make it an ideal choice for businesses looking for a more efficient payment solution.

    MoneyUnify is a trusted API integration platform used by businesses all over the world for mobile money payments. My goal on this project is to simplify the payment process and Make it easy for businesses to manage transactions from various mobile money networks.
